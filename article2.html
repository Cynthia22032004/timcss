How technology is revolutionizing homebuilding: From IoT to AI
echnology is changing industries worldwide, and homebuilding and residential construction are key examples.
T
In the last decade, innovations like the Internet of Things (IoT) and artificial intelligence (AI) have transformed residential construction. These advances have made homes smarter, more efficient, and better suited to the needs of their occupants. The global smart home market is projected to grow by 40% from 2022 to 2027, driven by advancements in technology and increasing consumer demand. 
Recent trends show how IoT devices, such as smart thermostats and automated security systems, have smoothly integrated into homebuilding, boosting convenience and energy management. At the same time, AI is increasingly crucial in improving design and construction processes, including predictive maintenance and advanced building simulations.
